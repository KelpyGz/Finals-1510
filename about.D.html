About Camping Paradise

Nestled deep within the pristine wilderness of the Oogie Boogie range, our scenic mountain retreat, founded in the 1800s, offers a captivating glimpse into a bygone era of rugged adventure and natural beauty.

Set against a backdrop of majestic peaks and dense pine forests, this historic campsite has been a sanctuary for nature enthusiasts for over a century.

Whether you seek solitude by a babbling mountain stream, thrilling hikes along trails carved out by early explorers, or simply a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of modern life, our mountain retreat beckons with its timeless allure, inviting you to create your own treasured memories in this picturesque wilderness retreat.
